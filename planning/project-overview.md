# Ozark Trail Section Hiking Project — Thread Summary

## Project Overview

Dan is section-hiking the full Ozark Trail (OT) in Missouri, with landscape and nature photography as a co-equal goal alongside the adventure. Based near St. Louis. The project is a multi-year journey broken into logical sections hiked 1-2 times per year.

## Key Constraints

- **Seasons to avoid:** Missouri summer heat/humidity, winter snow/ice
- **Hard stop:** Deer rifle season opens ~second Monday of November
- **Fall Spartan races:** Badlands Beast (end of Sept), Phoenix Sprint (weekend before Thanksgiving) — narrows fall backpacking window to mid-October through ~Nov 8
- **Target cadence:** 1 spring trip + 1 fall trip per year

## Fitness Baseline

Completed a 3-day/35-mile pack hike. Can comfortably do 15+ miles/day solo. Also competes in Spartan races.

## Taum Sauk Section — Decided Details

**Status:** Ready for dedicated planning chat

- **Direction:** North → South (Hwy 21 → Johnson's Shut-Ins State Park)
- **Timing:** Mid-to-late October for peak fall color
- **Duration:** 3 days
- **Photography priority:** Sunset camp positioning, slow final morning at Johnson's Shut-Ins
- **Terrain note:** OTA describes tread as "a natural stair-stepping machine" — plan for ~1mph slower than normal pace
- **Navigation warning:** Poor trail markers in rough sections, GPS essential, cell service unreliable except near power lines

**Suggested day splits:**

- Day 1: Hwy 21 → Bell Mountain area (~13 mi) — sunset camp
- Day 2: Bell Mtn → Taum Sauk summit → Mina Sauk Falls area (~13 mi)
- Day 3: Into Johnson's Shut-Ins finish (~9 mi) — slow/photography focus

**Camping rules:**

- Primitive dispersed, 100' from trail
- No camping on glades
- No camping within 2 miles of JSI main parking lot
- Backcountry registration required: mostateparks.com

**GPS/Maps:**

- GPX: https://ozarktrail.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/taumsauk_gps.zip
- PDF Map: https://ozarktrail.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/taum_sauk_maps.pdf
- Google Earth KMZ: https://ozarktrail.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/taumsauk_google.zip
