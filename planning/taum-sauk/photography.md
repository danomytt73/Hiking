# Camera Gear Planning — Session Summary

Photography kit planning for the OT Taum Sauk trip. Photography is a co-equal
goal alongside the hike itself.

---

## Decisions Made

- **One lens system:** RF 14-35mm f/4L only for OT Taum Sauk trip. No second lens.
- **On-the-move camera:** iPhone 17 Pro handles all hiking shots. R7 reserved for deliberate stationary setups.
- **Strap mounting retired:** R7 goes inside the pack. Peak Design aluminum capture clip won't be primary carry method.
- **Camera protection system:** Shimoda Small Core Unit or F-Stop Small ICU (camera insert/cube) inside the pack — lens stays attached at all times to minimize dust and mount wear.
- **Rain shooting:** Handled via a separate rain management thread — a dry/rain bag for shooting in wet conditions.
- **Polarizer:** 77mm slim circular polarizer needed for the RF 14-35mm. Top candidates: Breakthrough Photography X4 or B+W XS-Pro. Slim profile is critical to avoid vignetting at 14mm.
