# Ozark Trail — Planning

Planning documents for section-hiking the full **Ozark Trail (OT)** in Missouri,
with landscape and nature photography as a co-equal goal. The full OT is divided
into five logical sections tackled over multiple years; each section has its own
dedicated planning chat, captured here as markdown.

## Structure

Each hike section lives in its own folder under `planning/`. Cross-cutting
topics (gear, photography, meals, etc.) are captured within the relevant
section folder until they become general enough to promote to a shared folder.
